About Us

Welcome to Subhi Tour & Travels. Located in the heart of Indore at Radhika Kunj, we are dedicated to providing top-tier transportation solutions across India.

Founded by Kartik and Sourabh Dhakse, our mission is to make travel comfortable, affordable, and safe. Whether you need a luxury Innova for a family wedding, a comfortable Ertiga for a business trip, or a quick city ride, we have the fleet to serve you.

We specialize in both A/C and Non-A/C luxury car rentals with experienced drivers who know the routes inside out.
