Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

At Subhi Tour & Travels ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers ("you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website or booking services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to process your travel bookings, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, Phone Number, Email Address.
  • Trip Details: Pickup/Drop locations, dates, times, and travel preferences.
  • Vehicle Registration (for Partners): Vehicle numbers, model details, and owner contact info.
  • Driver Details (for Partners): License information, experience, and contact details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage your bookings.
  • To communicate with you regarding trip updates, driver details, or payment confirmations.
  • To improve our customer service and website experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., maintaining travel logs for police verification if required).

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share necessary details with:

  • Our Drivers: Sharing your name, phone number, and pickup location so the driver can locate you.
  • Law Enforcement: If required by law or to protect the safety of our drivers and vehicles.
  • WhatsApp: Since our booking system utilizes WhatsApp, data entered in forms is transmitted via the WhatsApp platform and is subject to their privacy policy.

4. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us directly via phone if you are uncomfortable sharing sensitive details online.

5. Cookies

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses standard web technologies. We may use basic cookies to ensure the website functions correctly. We do not use aggressive tracking cookies for advertising.
